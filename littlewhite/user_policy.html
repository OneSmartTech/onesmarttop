Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of July 19, 2024, and is issued by Chengdu One Smart Technology Co., LTD. ("OneSmart", "we", or "us"). It governs the collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you access or use our websites, mobile applications, and other online products and services (collectively, the "Services"), as well as when you interact with us through customer service, social media, or other means. By using the Services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using the Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

When registering for and/or using the Service, you may provide personal information such as your name, age, gender, email address, Audio, AI instructions (text or pictures input as AI prompts), and pictures for your profile avatar.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device and Usage Information: We collect details about your device, including your Internet protocol (IP) address, user settings, MAC address, cookie identifiers, mobile carrier, mobile advertising and other unique identifiers, browser or device information, general location information (derived from IP address and time zone), and Internet service provider. We also track your activities on the Service, such as the features you use and the time spent.

Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and web beacons to enhance your experience and collect information about your browsing habits. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

2.3 Information from Other Sources

Third-Party App Stores and Services: We may receive transaction information from app stores when you install or purchase subscriptions through them. Additionally, we might access your name and contact details from third-party calendar services if you integrate them with our Service.

Social Media Accounts: If you create or log into your account via a social media service, we will access certain information from that account, like your name and other profile details, following the authorization procedures of the social media platform.

Public and Partner Sources: We may obtain information from publicly available sources, marketing and advertising partners, consumer research platforms, and business contact databases. This information helps us understand our users better and improve our Services.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Provide and Improve the Services

We utilize your information to manage your account, provide access to Service features, and offer customer support. For example, if you encounter an issue, we use your contact details to assist you.

Audio analysis recognition is performed on the audio you input or upload. The analyzed results are used within the application to enhance your experience and help you better understand the audio content.

3.2 Administrative Purposes

We use your data for various administrative tasks, such as ensuring network security, preventing fraud, and measuring user engagement with our Services. This helps us improve and maintain the quality of our Services.

Short-term, transient use of your data, like customizing ads based on your interests, is done to provide a more personalized experience. However, we do not use or disclose health and fitness data for advertising or data mining purposes.

3.3 Marketing and Advertising

With your consent, we may use your information to offer personalized content and advertisements. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time. We respect your privacy and will not share your sensitive health and fitness data with third parties for marketing.

3.4 Other Purposes

If you consent, we may use your information for additional purposes disclosed at the time of collection.

We create de-identified and aggregated information from your data for analytical purposes. This helps us understand user trends and improve our Services without revealing your personal identity.

4. How We Disclose Your Information

4.1 Vendors and Service Providers

We share your information with trusted vendors and service providers who assist us in delivering the Services. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and security measures. For example, our IT support team helps maintain the Service infrastructure, and payment processors handle your transactions securely.

4.2 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will ensure that the receiving party adheres to the same privacy standards as us.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We disclose your information when required by law or to protect our rights, prevent fraud, or ensure the safety of our users and the public.

4.4 Other Users

Your profile information, such as your name and uploaded content, may be visible to other users to facilitate interaction within the Service. You can control the visibility of your information through privacy settings.

4.5 Advertising Partners

We may share non-sensitive information with third-party advertising partners to provide you with personalized ads. These partners use tracking technologies to understand your interests and deliver relevant advertisements. We do not share health and fitness data for advertising purposes.

5.1 Access and Control

You can review, update, or correct your personal information in the App's profile section. If you need a copy of your data or want to delete it, please contact us at hello@onesmart.tech. We will respond to your request within a reasonable time.

5.3 Age Limitation

We do not knowingly process personal data from individuals under 16 years of age. If you become aware of such data being provided, please inform us immediately.
